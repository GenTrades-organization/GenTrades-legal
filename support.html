Support

Need help with GenTrades? Here’s how to get support.

Contact us

For account issues, bugs, or questions about the app, email us at dane@gentrades.app. We aim to respond as soon as possible. Please include your account email and a short description so we can help you faster.

Account and subscription

You can manage your profile and delete your account from Settings in the app. To cancel or manage a subscription, use your device settings: on iOS go to Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions; on Android use the Play Store subscription management. Use Restore purchases in the app if you reinstall or switch devices.
